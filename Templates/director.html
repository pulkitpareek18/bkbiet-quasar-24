A MESSAGE FROM

THE DIRECTOR'S DESK

Dr. S M Prasanna Kumar

Director, BKBIET Pilani

The value of sports is something we can't overlook. In today’s world, where technology and automation are advancing rapidly, we've gained a lot, but we've also lost touch with some essential habits—particularly those that keep us physically and mentally healthy. It’s becoming more important than ever to focus on our bodies and minds, rather than just getting caught up in the digital world. And sports are one of the best ways to help us make that shift. Whether it's something as simple as boosting our mood with a rush of dopamine, or more profound benefits like building teamwork, discipline, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle, sports are an irreplaceable part of living a well-rounded life.

BKBIET Pilani has always been committed to the holistic development of its students, recognizing the importance of sports in shaping not just physical strength but also mental resilience and teamwork. It is with great pleasure that I welcome the return of QUASAR 2024, our Annual Sports Fest, after a brief hiatus. Sports are an essential part of life, and here at BKBIET, we view QUASAR as one of the most significant events on our calendar. It provides a unique platform for students to showcase their athletic talents, push their limits, and celebrate the true spirit of sportsmanship.

It is with great enthusiasm that I welcome you to QUASAR 2024, the Annual Sports Fest of BKBIET Pilani, set to take place from the 29th of November to the 1st of December. As we gather once again for this vibrant celebration of sportsmanship, competition, and camaraderie, I am confident that this year's fest will be yet another remarkable chapter in the history of QUASAR. Each year, QUASAR serves as a platform for our students to showcase their athletic abilities, push their limits, and embrace the spirit of fair competition. This year's edition promises to be no different, as we come together to witness thrilling contests across a wide array of sports and events. Whether you're participating or cheering on your peers, the energy and enthusiasm of this event will be unparalleled. With the theme "Unleash the Champion Within," we encourage all our athletes to rise to the occasion, giving their best in every match, every game, and every event. This is a celebration of not just physical strength, but also the determination, perseverance, and teamwork that make sports truly inspiring.

As the legendary Sachin Tendulkar once said, “People throw stones at you and you convert them into milestones.” This quote embodies the essence of what sports is all about — facing challenges head-on, turning adversity into success, and always striving for greatness. Let this spirit guide all our participants, as you rise to the occasion, embrace every challenge, and strive to reach new heights in your athletic journey.

I extend my best wishes to the organizers and the participants for a great Meet.
