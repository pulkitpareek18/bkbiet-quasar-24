ABOUT US

Welcome to Quasar, the premier sports festival at B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering and Technology (BKBIET)! A celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and boundless energy, Quasar brings together students from across disciplines for an unforgettable sporting experience. Every year, our campus transforms into a vibrant arena of competition, camaraderie, and triumph.

Quasar isn’t just about winning; it’s about pushing boundaries, breaking limits, and embodying the true spirit of sportsmanship. With a wide range of events across various sports, including football, basketball, cricket, Volleyball, and more, we provide a platform for every student to showcase their skills and passion. Whether you’re a player or a spectator, Quasar promises to be an event like no other.

At Quasar, we believe in fostering a sense of unity and belonging. It’s a celebration of talent, dedication, and the thrill of competition. Every game, every cheer, and every victory strengthens the bond between participants, creating lasting memories and lifelong friendships.

Join us at Quasar, where the spirit of sport meets the energy of B.K. Birla Institute of Engineering and Technology. Come for the competition, stay for the excitement, and leave with a lifetime of memories!

THE DIRECTOR'S DESK

Dr. S M Prasanna Kumar

Director, BKBIET Pilani

It is with great enthusiasm that I welcome you to QUASAR 2024, the Annual Sports Fest of BKBIET Pilani, set to take place from the 29th of November to the 1st of December. As we gather once again for this vibrant celebration of sportsmanship, competition, and camaraderie, I am confident that this year's fest will be yet another remarkable chapter in the history of QUASAR. Each year, QUASAR serves as a platform for our students to showcase their athletic abilities, push their limits, and embrace the spirit of fair competition. This year's edition promises to be no different, as we come together to witness thrilling contests across a wide array of sports and events. Whether you're participating or cheering on your peers, the energy and enthusiasm of this event will be unparalleled. With the theme "Unleash the Champion Within," we encourage all our athletes to rise to the occasion, giving their best in every match, every game, and every event. This is a celebration of not just physical strength, but also the determination, perseverance, and teamwork that make sports truly inspiring. I extend my best wishes to all the participants, and I am certain that QUASAR 2024 will be a fantastic success. Let us come together to celebrate the power of sport and the indomitable spirit of BKBIET!

I extend my best wishes to the organizers and the participants for a great Meet.
